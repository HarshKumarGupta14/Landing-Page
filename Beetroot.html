"BEETROOT"

Beetroot, also known as table beet or garden beet, is a root vegetable with a deep red or purple color. It's a popular ingredient in salads, juices, and various dishes due to its earthy and slightly sweet flavor. Beetroot is also a good source of nutrients like folate, manganese, and potassium, and is known for its potential health benefits, including improved blood flow and athletic performance.

Key Characteristics:

  • Scientific Name: Beta vulgaris.
  • Color: Typically deep red or purple, but can also be golden or even striped (like candy cane beets).
  • Flavor: Earthy and slightly sweet.
  • Nutritional Value: Rich in folate, manganese, potassium, iron, and vitamin C.
  • Culinary Uses: Salads, juices, soups, roasted dishes, and even in desserts.
